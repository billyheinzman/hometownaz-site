📅 What's happening

A community calendar with something almost every day

Between the Sage Recreation Center, the Saguaro Recreation Center, the Copper Canyon golf clubhouse, and dozens of chartered clubs, Sun City Festival residents have access to a packed activity schedule covered by the master HOA. The full current calendar lives on the official HOA site — and we've curated the categories below so you know what kinds of things to expect.

⭐ Official source · Always current
Sun City Festival Community Association Calendar
The HOA maintains the master calendar of events at oursuncityfestival.net — including dated events, club meetings, fitness classes, and tournaments. This is the single source of truth for what's happening this week and this month.

What kinds of events happen here?

Festival's event mix runs from quiet morning yoga to community-wide concerts in the Sage ballroom. Here are the categories you'll see on any given month's calendar:

🎵
Concerts & Performances
Live music, tribute acts, and community concerts in the Sage Center ballroom. Holiday concerts, summer pool-deck music, and seasonal special events.
💃
Dances & Social Events
Ballroom dances, line dancing nights, themed parties, and casual mixers. Held in the ballroom or by individual chartered clubs.
⛳
Golf Tournaments
Regular member tournaments at Copper Canyon Golf Club — scrambles, leagues, charity events. Plus public-play tournament events open to all golfers.
💪
Fitness Classes
Daily group fitness — yoga, water aerobics, strength training, low-impact cardio, Pilates. Most classes are included; a few specialty instructors have a nominal per-class fee.
👥
Chartered Club Meetings
Festival has dozens of resident-run chartered clubs — quilting, woodworking, billiards, photography, book clubs, investment, RV travel, and more. Each has its own meeting schedule.
🎨
Craft Workshops & Classes
Held mainly at the Saguaro Center — ceramics studio sessions, painting classes, jewelry-making, scrapbooking. Materials fees may apply for consumables.
🎓
Lectures & Speakers
Travel talks, financial planning seminars, health & wellness lectures, history presentations — typically free for residents.
🍳
Restaurant Events
Special menus, happy hours, and themed nights at Indigo Grille at the Falls (the Copper Canyon clubhouse restaurant). Open to the general public.
🎄
Seasonal & Holiday Events
Halloween parties, Veterans Day ceremonies, Thanksgiving dinners, December holiday celebrations, New Year's Eve dances, July 4th cookouts.
