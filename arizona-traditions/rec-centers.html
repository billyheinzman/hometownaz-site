🏊 The Complete Amenity & Activity Guide

A small-community feel with big-community amenities

Arizona Traditions was deliberately built smaller than the Sun City master-planned communities — but with full-scale amenities. A 20,400 sqft clubhouse anchors the community, paired with an 18-hole golf course, 3 pools, dedicated studios for crafts and woodworking, and 40+ active clubs.

🏛️ The Clubhouse

The 20,400-square-foot clubhouse is the central hub with a 700-person ballroom, dedicated studios for arts/crafts/ceramics/sewing/stained glass/woodworking, fitness center, library, hobby/game rooms, and a billiards room. The Arizona Traditions Bar (attached to the clubhouse) is where residents gather after a round of golf or for casual dinners.

⛳ Golf

The 18-hole Arizona Traditions Golf Course, designed by Dick Bailey, winds through the community. Playable for all skill levels. Privately owned but open to the public with resident discounts.
