📍 Fun Things Locally!

Arizona Traditions local guide — restaurants, healthcare, and the things residents actually do

Arizona Traditions is positioned at the western edge of Surprise, just inside the city limits on the way to Buckeye. Most daily needs are within a 5-10 minute drive on Bell Road or Reems Road. Below: verified, currently-operating restaurants, bars, shops, and healthcare facilities residents actually use (with ratings and review counts from Google), plus where to drive 15-20 minutes for a special night out. Healthcare is close — Abrazo Surprise Hospital is one mile away on Bell Road.

🍽️ Right next door — Chef Peter's at The Chuckwagon

Chef Peter's sits directly outside the Arizona Traditions main gate at 17200 W Bell Rd. It's the closest sit-down restaurant to the community — a casual breakfast/lunch/early-dinner spot where residents go for the corned beef, patty melts, steak bites, and BBQ-style mains. ⭐4.5 from 51 reviews. Open Tues–Sun (closed Mondays).

This is the spot for "let's grab a quick bite without driving anywhere." Many residents are regulars and know Chef Peter by name.

🍷 Sit-down dinner spots residents drive 5-10 minutes for

  • Bonfire Craft Kitchen and Tap House — ⭐4.5 (3,182 reviews) — One of the most popular sit-down dinner spots in Surprise. BBQ, pizza, large craft beer menu, DJ Bingo nights. 15332 W Bell Rd · (623) 295-4567. About 4 miles east of Arizona Traditions.
  • Firebirds Wood Fired Grill — ⭐4.3 (1,010 reviews) — Wood-fired steakhouse, reservations recommended, white tablecloth feel. 13388 N Prasada Pkwy · (602) 922-9899. The date-night option.
  • The Toast Craft Kitchen & Cocktails — ⭐4.3 (449 reviews) — Small, locally-owned, the kind of place where servers know regulars by name. Mediterranean chicken, lamb bolognese, lump crab cakes, an espresso martini residents talk about. Closed Sundays. 17058 W Bell Rd Suite 104 · (623) 440-4503.
  • Osteria Mia — ⭐4.4 (114 reviews) — New Italian spot, made-from-scratch pastas, focaccia, attentive service. 16853 N 145th Ave Suite 155 · (602) 897-5470. About 4 miles east.
  • Maple & Rye — ⭐4.1 (400 reviews) — Bar & grill with brunch, eggs Benedict, spicy bloody marys. 15955 W Clearview Blvd · (623) 271-9400. Open daily 8am–8pm.

🥞 Breakfast & casual lunch spots

  • Over Easy — ⭐4.4 (1,363 reviews) — One of the most popular breakfast spots in Surprise. Featured on TV cooking shows, lines on weekends. Open 6:30am–2pm daily. 14345 W Bell Rd · (623) 230-2160.
  • Chef Peter's at The Chuckwagon — Breakfast and lunch, just outside the AZ Traditions main gate. See above.
  • Maple & Rye — Breakfast served all day, brunch on weekends.
  • sneakybird — ⭐4.6 (476 reviews) — Baked (not fried) chicken tenders, wings, sliders, sweet potato tots. Healthier fast-casual option, mom-and-pop family-owned. 16430 W Waddell Rd #108 · (623) 220-5444.

🍻 Bars, breweries, and live entertainment

  • Arizona Traditions Bar — Inside the community itself, attached to the clubhouse. Where residents gather after golf or for casual dinners. Hours and menu via the community office.
  • O.H.S.O. Brewery — Surprise — ⭐4.2 (540 reviews) — Craft brewery and gastropub, dog-friendly patio with its own dog menu, open until midnight Fri/Sat. 13448 N Prasada Pkwy · (623) 307-7001.

  • Quench Taps and Tapas — ⭐4.6 (342 reviews) — Wine bar and small-plates spot with charcuterie boards. Sophisticated vibe, semi-vintage decor, not for kids. 14010 N Litchfield Rd Ste 212 · (480) 670-2962. Closed Mon/Sun.
  • Copper + Rye — ⭐4.4 (161 reviews) — Beautifully designed space, girls' night out spot. 11530 N Greenwich Blvd · (623) 213-7000.

  • Safeway — ⭐4.2 (1,762 reviews) — Full-service grocery, pharmacy, deli, bakery. 17049 W Bell Rd · (623) 518-1059. Right across the street from WinCo.
  • Fry's Marketplace — ⭐4.2 (1,027 reviews) — Kroger-family grocery, larger format with general merchandise. 15510 W Bell Rd · (623) 546-2522.
  • Trader Joe's — ⭐4.7 (2,766 reviews) — The cult-favorite specialty grocer for cheese, wine, frozen meals, flowers. 14095 W Grand Ave · (623) 546-1640. About 6 miles east.

🏥 Healthcare — one mile away

Abrazo Surprise Hospital is the closest full hospital to Arizona Traditions — at 16815 W Bell Rd, about a one-mile drive from the community gate. Full 24-hour ER, hospital beds, and surgical services. ⭐3.3 from 282 reviews — mixed but improved recent reviews. (623) 244-2400.

For more complex care or specialty services, residents go to:

  • Banner Del E. Webb Medical Center — ⭐3.4 (1,187 reviews) — Full-service hospital, senior specialty services, one of the West Valley's major medical centers. 14502 W Meeker Blvd, Sun City West · (623) 524-4000. About 10 minutes east.
  • HonorHealth Complete Care – Surprise — ⭐3.9 (752 reviews) — 24-hour urgent care/ER hybrid. 16575 W Waddell Rd · (623) 300-9110.
  • Arizona General Hospital ER — Surprise — ⭐3.6 (331 reviews) — Smaller, faster-moving emergency facility. 14267 W Bell Rd · (623) 546-5230.

Multiple primary care offices, urgent cares, and pharmacies sit along Bell Road within a 3-mile radius. This is one of the strong selling points of Arizona Traditions vs more isolated communities — when something happens, you're minutes from real care.

⚾ Sports & outdoor recreation

  • Surprise Stadium (Spring Training) — ⭐4.8 (110 reviews) — Spring home of the Kansas City Royals and Texas Rangers. February–March MLB exhibition games right in Surprise. 15960 N Bullard Ave · (623) 222-2222. About 5 miles east of Arizona Traditions.
  • White Tank Mountain Regional Park — ⭐4.8 (397 reviews) — 30,000+ acres of desert hiking, the famous waterfall trail, petroglyphs, camping, picnic ramadas. Right at the foot of the mountains visible from Arizona Traditions. 20304 W White Tank Mountain Rd, Waddell · (602) 506-2930. About 10 miles southwest.
  • Surprise Community Park — ⭐4.7 (3,535 reviews) — Large public park with pickleball, tennis, fishing pond, dog park, jungle gyms, walking paths. Christmas celebration with fireworks every year. 16089 N Bullard Ave.
  • Surprise Tennis & Racquet Complex — ⭐4.5 (228 reviews) — World-class tennis facility, USTA National venue. Public access. 14469 W Paradise Ln · (623) 222-2400.
  • Surprise Community Park Pickleball Courts — Public courts, leagues, tournaments. Heavy use during snowbird season; off-season is much easier to get a court.

🛍️ Bigger shopping & entertainment — 15-20 minute drives

For department stores, movie theaters, full malls, and bigger entertainment, residents head east on Bell Road or south on Loop 303:

  • Surprise Towne Center & Marketplace — ⭐4.3 (4,100 reviews) — Shopping center at Bell Rd & Grand Ave with Fry's, Home Depot, Black Rock, restaurants, Sephora, Claire's, big-box stores. W. Bell Rd & W Grand Ave.
  • Prasada — Newer outdoor shopping/dining district with OHSO Brewery, Firebirds, Copper + Rye, sneakybird. About 5 miles east at Loop 303 & Waddell Rd.
  • Arrowhead Towne Center — ⭐4.4 (14,930 reviews) — Two-story enclosed mall, Glendale. Department stores, food court, indoor walking when it's hot. 7700 W Arrowhead Towne Center, Glendale · (623) 979-7777. About 25 minutes east.
  • Westgate Entertainment District — ⭐4.6 (20,219 reviews) — Glendale's big entertainment district. State Farm Stadium (Cardinals), Coyotes hockey arena (now Mullett Arena territory), Yard House, AMC theater, fountains. 6770 N Sunrise Blvd, Glendale · (623) 266-6693. About 30 minutes east.

📍 Map & logistics — how Arizona Traditions is laid out

Arizona Traditions sits at 17221 N Citrus Rd, just west of the 303 Loop in Surprise, at the base of the White Tank Mountains. The main gate is on Citrus Rd, off Bell Rd.

  • Bell Road (southern boundary corridor) — your main artery for groceries, restaurants, healthcare. Almost everything you need daily is within 1-2 miles east on Bell.
  • Loop 303 (5 minutes east) — the major freeway. Connects you to I-10 south (Phoenix in 45 min) and Loop 101 east (Glendale, Westgate, downtown Phoenix).
  • Reems Road (parallel commercial corridor to the north) — secondary retail strip.

Sky Harbor airport is ~50 minutes via Loop 303 → I-10. Phoenix Deer Valley Airport is closer (~35 min) if you fly private.

Arizona Traditions is intentionally on the quieter, western edge of the Surprise commercial area — meaning lower traffic noise, easier parking, but a couple extra minutes to most retail. That's a feature, not a bug, for most residents who came here for the small-town feel.
