Sun City has 130+ chartered clubs and a calendar of community events that would exhaust someone half the age of most residents. The official RCSC calendar lives at suncityaz.org; we're building a curated overview here with the can't-miss events and how to plug in if you're new.

📆 Marquee annual events

Sun City Founders Day (early January, anniversary celebration), Sun Bowl concert series (Oct-April), Spring Fling (March arts & crafts festival), RCSC Annual Membership Meeting (where you can actually vote on community policy), and the quarterly New Resident Orientation at Bell Rec Center.

🧩 How to plug in as a new resident

(1) Pick up the RCSC quarterly amenity guide at any rec center. (2) Attend a New Resident Orientation — they run quarterly. (3) Walk into any rec center and ask a desk clerk about the clubs — they'll hand you a list. (4) Show up at one club meeting; you'll be invited to 3 more by the end of it. The community is famously friendly to newcomers.
