About Westbrook Village

Westbrook Village is one of the few age-restricted communities in the West Valley with a 40+ age minimum (instead of 55+), making it a popular choice for early retirees and younger active adults. Located on the north edge of the original Sun City, the community covers a 2-square-mile area with 3,910 homes spread across 44 distinct enclaves. Built between 1982 and 1993 by UDC; no new construction remains.

Community Highlights

  • 40+ age minimum (vs 55+ at neighboring communities) — the lowest age restriction of any major West Valley active-adult community
  • 36 holes of golf: Lakes Course (par 71) and Vistas Course (par 72)
  • Two recreation centers with full amenities (residents on both sides have access to both)
  • Lower HOA fees than the Sun Cities — some neighborhoods around $50/month
  • Easy 101 freeway access: downtown Phoenix in 30 minutes, Arrowhead Mall 2 miles away
  • Resale-only: all homes built 1982–1993; mature trees, established character
