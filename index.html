Which Del Webb community is right for me?

It depends on what you're looking for. Each of the four Del Webb communities in Arizona's West Valley has its own personality:

Sun City (1960) is the original — friendliest prices, most established trees, 55+. Sun City West (1978–1997) offers larger lots and upgraded construction, 55+ with seven golf courses. Sun City Grand (1996–2005) is the modern flagship — four championship golf courses, 45+ age policy. Corte Bella (2003–2007) is the gated boutique option — only 1,650 homes, country-club lifestyle.

Use the four community cards above to start exploring, or call us at (623) 203-6271 and we'll walk you through it personally.

Do you help with out-of-state relocation to Arizona?

Yes — this is one of our specialties. Billy leads our out-of-state relocation program with virtual tours, video walkthroughs, detailed neighborhood insights, and responsive remote communication. Lona provides deep local knowledge so every long-distance buyer fully understands the community, HOA rules, fees, and lifestyle before committing.

We've successfully closed homes for buyers who never set foot in Arizona before their move-in day. Reach out and we'll explain exactly how we handle remote transactions.
