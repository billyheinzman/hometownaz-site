A little "piece of paradise" in the West Valley

Sun Village is a boutique, gated 55+ community tucked into the heart of Surprise — 1,382 homes and single-level condos wrapped around sparkling lakes and an award-winning golf course. It pairs resort-style amenities with a small-town, lock-and-leave feel. The community sits steps from Surprise Stadium (spring-training home of the Texas Rangers and Kansas City Royals), a 25-court tennis & racquet complex, the Maricopa Regional Library, an aquatic center, dog parks, and a fishing lake. Between the on-site amenities and everything just outside the gates, there's rarely a reason to leave.

The on-site restaurant, salon, gift gallery, golf course, and community church are open to the public; the rest of the community is private with guard-controlled entry.
