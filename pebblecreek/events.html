PebbleCreek Events Calendar

PebbleCreek doesn't just host events — many have run for decades. The Holiday Golf Cart Parade, the Memorial Day Pool Party, Tour de PebbleCreek, the Veterans Day Parade — they're part of how the community marks the year together.

SIGNATURE ANNUAL EVENTS

Marquee community traditions

🎄

Holiday Golf Cart Parade

Annual December tradition. Residents deck out their golf carts in holiday-inspired decor — some dress to match. Featured by local news (Fox 10, ABC 15) most years.

DEC · WHOLE COMMUNITY
🏊

Memorial Day Celebration Pool Party

The resort-style pool complex turns into an island paradise. Evening event with socializing, dancing, and full use of the Tuscany Sports & Aquatics outdoor pool.

LATE MAY · TUSCANY POOL
🚴

Tour de PebbleCreek

Coincides with the Tour de France. Cycling enthusiasts ride one mile for every ten in the actual Tour, logging 206 miles total over 21 days. Multi-week event.

JUL · CYCLING CLUB
🇺🇸

Veterans Day Parade

Annual recognition of military personnel — special guests, speakers, patriotism, and gratitude. The Corvette Club leads an honor ride.

NOV 11 · COMMUNITY-WIDE
🚗

PebbleCreek Autofest

Annual classic car show run by the Classics Car Club. Donates food and money to the Agua Fria Food Bank and other local charities.

ANNUAL · CLASSICS CAR CLUB
⛳

Golf Tournaments (Year-Round)

Multiple tournaments throughout the year hosted by the various golf associations and clubs. Some are association-internal, others are charity benefits.

YEAR-ROUND · ALL ASSOC.
🔥

Backyard BBQ Brawl

Cross-community Robson event. All five Robson Resort Communities compete for BBQ bragging rights. PebbleCreek hosts/competes annually.

FALL · MULTI-COMMUNITY

WEEKLY & MONTHLY EVENTS

What's happening any given week

Beyond the big annual traditions, PebbleCreek has a packed weekly calendar:

  • Bridge: 6 days a week (casual, marathon, sanctioned and non-sanctioned duplicate)
  • Pickleball: Daily open play across 36 courts plus club tournaments
  • Group fitness classes: Multiple daily at the Tuscany Fitness Studios (yoga, aqua, spin, line dancing, zumba, kettlebell, strength)
  • Bocce: 3 seasonal sessions (Sept – mid-May)
  • Open Beading Group: Mondays 1pm at Creative Arts Center
  • Bingo: Weekly evening gatherings
  • Tennis matches and clinics: Multiple weekly
  • Camera Club, Art Club, Ceramics, Quilters, Sewing — most meet weekly at the Creative Arts Center
  • Renaissance Theater: Concerts, plays, seminars, performances throughout the season
  • Westwind Tavern: Live music, bar trivia, and other scheduled entertainment

Where to find the current schedule

The community publishes its detailed calendar through several channels:

  • PebbleCreek Post Newspaper — monthly newspaper with all upcoming events. pebblecreekpost.com →
  • PebbleTV — community TV channel. pebbletv.org →
  • Robson PebbleCreek Blog — news, event recaps, and updates. robson.com →
  • Community bulletin boards — posted in each clubhouse and at the gates
  • Club newsletters — each club publishes its own schedule
💡 Insider Tip

When are buyers most likely to attend an event?

If you're considering PebbleCreek, the Holiday Golf Cart Parade in December is a great event for prospective buyers — you'll see hundreds of residents out, get a feel for the community vibe, and walk through neighborhoods you might not otherwise visit. The Memorial Day Pool Party is similar in May.

Also: Preferred Guest Program visits from Robson are timed around busy event weeks — they want you to see the community at its liveliest. Call us if you'd like advice on which dates align best with seeing PebbleCreek in full swing.
