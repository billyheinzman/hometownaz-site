Recreation Centers at PebbleCreek

PebbleCreek's amenities aren't a single clubhouse — they're spread across six major facilities covering 100+ activities. Two clubhouses, three pool complexes, three fitness studios, a creative arts center, and an event theater. Here's what each facility houses and where to find it inside the gates.

FACILITY #1

Tuscany Sports & Aquatics Complex

The flagship facility, centrally located. This is where most residents spend the bulk of their amenity time — fitness, pools, racquet sports, all in one connected complex.

🏊

Indoor Lap Pool

Heated indoor pool with lap lanes. Year-round swimming — especially valuable in winter when outdoor pools aren't as comfortable.

🏖️

Outdoor Resort-Style Pool

Beach-entry access, in-pool seating, plus a resistance walking channel. Covered shaded areas for the Arizona afternoon sun.

🏊‍♂️

Outdoor Lap Pool & Whirlpool

Lap pool for fitness swimming plus a whirlpool spa for relaxation/recovery.

👶

Grandkids Pool

Designed specifically for small children. Visiting grandkids stay nearby while you're poolside.

💆

Massage Rooms

On-site therapeutic massage treatments — you don't need to leave the community for a massage appointment.

🏓

Pickleball Complex

36 courts — one of the largest pickleball complexes in Arizona. Recent 16-court expansion completed in 2022.

Largest in AZ for 55+
🎾

Tennis Courts

7 tennis courts available for residents at Tuscany. (Eagle's Nest has 6 additional lighted courts.)

🎯

Bocce Courts

12 bocce courts. The Bocce Ball Association runs 3 sessions a year (September through mid-May), all skill levels welcome.

💪

State-of-the-Art Fitness Center

Separate cardio and weight rooms. Cardio equipment with internet/cable connectivity, full free weights, LifeFitness machines.

☕

Cool Down Café

Inside the fitness center. Smoothies, energy drinks, fresh fruit, power bars — for after a workout or round of golf.

FACILITY #2

Tuscany Fitness Studios

Three dedicated studios for instructor-led classes, separate from the main fitness center floor.

🧘

Studio #1 — The Main Room

2,500 sq ft. Instructor-led group classes up to 50 students. Includes WellBeats virtual fitness on demand (kickboxing, vibe, definitions, stomp, etc.).

💃

Studio #2 — Group & Dance

1,500 sq ft. Group exercise (up to 30 students). Doubles as dance studio for the Dance Fusion performance group and ShowTime theater group rehearsals.

🚴

Studio #3 — Spin & Yoga

900 sq ft. 12 spin bikes, 60" TV/DVD system. Also used for stretching and yoga.

The studios are scheduled with a published group fitness calendar — kettlebell, yoga, strength, aqua, classic aerobics, spin, zumba, line dancing, and others. The schedule rotates seasonally and the community publishes it monthly. Most classes are no extra fee — included with your HOA dues.

FACILITY #3

Tuscany Clubhouse

The community's social and event hub. Rustic-elegant design, hosts special events, performances, and the daily social activity calendar.

🎭

Renaissance Theater

332 seats. Plays, musicals, seminars, comedy, variety shows, lectures. Home to the Community Players, PebbleCreek Singers, and ShowTime Productions.

💃

Ballroom / Auditorium

Large ballroom with stage for dinner-dance events, dance club socials, expo-style club showcases, and formal/casual private events.

🎱

Billiards Room

Refined and elegant setting for a friendly game of pool. Open to all residents.

📚

Library

Grand fireplace at center, comfy chairs, surrounding bookcases — a warm, quiet space to read or work.

🚪

Multi-Purpose Activity Rooms

Smaller, more intimate rooms for club meetings, classes, board games, and special-interest groups.

☕

Portofino Coffee Bar & Lounge

In the Tuscany Clubhouse. Regular and specialty coffee, lattes, freshly baked warm pastries. The morning gathering spot.

FACILITY #4

Oasis Pool Complex

A second resort-style pool complex on the southwest side of the community. Quieter than Tuscany, more park-like with stately palms and lush landscaping.

🏊

Resort-Style Pool

Sparkling resort-style pool with in-pool seating and shaded chaise lounge areas.

⛺

Covered Ramada

Seats up to 36 people. Reservable in advance for private parties and gatherings.

🔥

BBQ Grills

Gas grills available for poolside BBQ — bring your own food or grab takeout from Ed's Dogs.

♿

Accessibility

Disability chair lift, 2 outdoor shower stalls (one handicap-equipped), accessible restrooms.

FACILITY #5

Creative Arts Center

Dedicated building for the visual and hand-craft clubs. Equipped studios — not just rooms, but actual purpose-built workshops.

🏺

Ceramics Studio

On-site kiln for pottery and sculpture firing. Equipped for creating, designing, and firing in one space.

🎨

Painting Studio

Easels, tables, supplies — dedicated to painting. Home to the Art Club (PCAC).

🧵

Sewing, Quilting & Crafts

Multi-room area for fiber arts, sewing, stitching, beading, quilting, stained glass, fused glass.

🪵

Woodcarving Studio

Fully outfitted with carving tools and equipment. Home to the Woodcarvers Club (20-25 classes per October-April season).

🖼️

Expressions Gallery

Consignment outlet for resident artisans — sketching, painting, ceramics, glasswork, jewelry. Sell what you create here.

FACILITY #6

Eagle's Nest Clubhouse

The original community clubhouse, co-located with Eagle's Nest Golf Course. Smaller than Tuscany but full-featured — and home to the second fitness center, the third pool, and the lighted tennis courts.

🏊

Resort-Style Pool & Whirlpool

Third pool complex in the community. Outdoor resort-style — the relaxed alternative to Tuscany's busier scene.

🎾

Six Lighted Tennis Courts

Lighted for night play — important during Arizona summers when daytime tennis is too hot. These are in addition to the 7 Tuscany courts.

💪

Fitness Center

Cardio and weight machines. Smaller than the main Tuscany center but typically less crowded.

🎭

Grand Ballroom & Auditorium

Hosts large-scale dinner/dance events, expos, and club activities. Alternate event venue to Tuscany.

📚

Library & Billiards Room

Library with grand fireplace, comfy chairs, bookcases. Fully stocked billiards room (pool cues, balls).

🍔

Eagle's Nest Restaurant & Lounge

Comfort foods: pot roast, chicken parmesan, burgers, sandwiches, wraps. The casual dining alternative to Westwind Tavern.

💡 Insider Tip

Which side of the community fits you?

PebbleCreek is large enough that location within the community matters. Here's how we coach buyers:

If you want to be near Tuscany action: Look at homes closer to the center of the community. You'll walk to Tuscany Sports & Aquatics, the Tuscany Clubhouse, the Renaissance Theater, the new Westwind Tavern, and the Creative Arts Center. Most clubs meet here.

If you want quieter and Eagle's Nest-centered: Homes on the Eagle's Nest side are more residential, less event-driven. You're still 5 minutes from Tuscany by golf cart, but daily life is calmer. The Eagle's Nest fitness center and pool are less crowded.

If you want the Oasis Pool vibe: Homes in the southwest area are closest to the Oasis Pool Complex — quieter, more park-like, with the BBQ ramada nearby.

Most residents keep a golf cart to get between facilities — and around the community generally. The streets are golf-cart friendly and it's the dominant way locals move.
