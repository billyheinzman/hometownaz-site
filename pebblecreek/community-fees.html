PebbleCreek Community Fees

PebbleCreek is a Robson Resort Community — and like other Robson properties, it operates on a homeowner association model with monthly dues that cover amenity access, gate/security, common-area maintenance, and golf operations. Here's a structured guide to every fee a PebbleCreek homeowner pays, what it covers, and how it compares to neighbors.

🏘️ HOA Master Association Dues

~$214/moApprox, 2026

The PebbleCreek HOA bills monthly for the master association. Dues cover:

  • Common-area landscaping and maintenance
  • Gate operation and 24-hour security
  • Access to all six amenity facilities (Tuscany Sports & Aquatics, Tuscany Clubhouse, Tuscany Fitness Studios, Oasis Pool, Creative Arts Center, Eagle's Nest Clubhouse)
  • Most group fitness classes and club use
  • Pool maintenance (all three pools)
  • Reserve contributions for major capital projects

Note: Golf is generally a pay-per-round model on top of HOA dues (no separate required golf membership). Pickleball, tennis, fitness center, all included. Some specialty clubs charge small annual dues separately.

💰 New Buyer One-Time Fees

VariesAt closing

When you buy a PebbleCreek resale home, you'll pay several one-time fees at closing:

  • Transfer Fee — paid to the HOA. Covers admin costs of the ownership change.
  • Capital Contribution / Working Capital — one-time contribution to the HOA reserves. Typical of master-planned communities.
  • Resale Disclosure / Title fees — standard Arizona fees, not specific to PebbleCreek but required.
  • Property Tax Pro-rations — split between buyer/seller based on closing date.

Combined, expect roughly $1,500-$3,000+ in one-time HOA-related fees at closing for a typical resale purchase. Call us for an exact estimate on a specific home — these add up.

🏛️ Property Tax

~0.7-1.0%Of assessed

Goodyear/Maricopa County property tax rates run roughly 0.7-1.0% of the assessed value annually, depending on bond levies and special districts. For a $600,000 home, expect roughly $4,200-$6,000/year, billed in two installments (October and March).

Arizona has a homestead-style "primary residence" cap that limits property tax increases year-over-year — important if you're moving from California or another high-tax state. Talk to the Maricopa County Assessor or a local tax professional about the specifics.

⛳ Golf Fees (Per Round)

Pay-as-you-goNo mandatory membership

Unlike some retirement communities (where a golf club membership is required separately or mandatory if you live in a golf villa), PebbleCreek has no required golf membership fee for residents. You pay per round at resident rates, which are well below daily green fees for public play.

Current detailed rate sheets are available at both pro shops (Tuscany Falls 623-935-6757 / Eagle's Nest 623-935-6761). Rates vary by season (peak winter rates, lower summer rates), tee time (early/twilight), and walking vs cart.

💼 Other Recurring Costs

VariesOptional
  • Homeowner's insurance — varies. Standard AZ rates. PebbleCreek's solid construction and low flood risk help.
  • Utilities — APS (electric), SW Gas, Liberty Utilities (water), regular trash. Average all-in: $200-450/month depending on home size and AC usage.
  • Solar lease/owned — if the home has solar, you'll inherit the lease or own the panels. See our solar guide.
  • Specialty club dues — most clubs free or small annual dues ($20-$100). Some specialty groups (camera, art, wine) charge nominal dues to fund supplies.
  • RV parking lease — if you have an RV and want to lease the offsite community lot, that's a separate monthly cost.
💡 Honest Comparison

How PebbleCreek's fees compare

Compared to Corte Bella (semi-private golf, similar zip): PebbleCreek's HOA dues are similar overall. Corte Bella has its C.A.R.E. fee (one-time, ~$5,000 at closing) plus annual HOA. PebbleCreek's one-time costs are typically lower at closing.

Compared to Sun City West / Sun City Grand: These are older communities with much lower HOA dues (often $1,200-$1,800/year vs PebbleCreek's $214/month or ~$2,500/year). The trade-off: Sun City West's amenities are older, and the golf is on a separate per-round basis. PebbleCreek delivers a more resort-grade amenity set for the higher fee.

Compared to Robson Ranch (sister property): Similar HOA structure, similar fee level. Robson Ranch is north of Phoenix near Eloy; PebbleCreek is in the West Valley.
