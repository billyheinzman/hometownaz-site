📍 About the Community

PebbleCreek, Goodyear AZ — Robson's luxury resort flagship

PebbleCreek is Robson Communities' flagship 55+ luxury retirement community, located in Goodyear about 20 miles west of downtown Phoenix. Construction began in 1993 and continues today. The original Eagle's Nest neighborhood preserves the established character, while the newer Tuscany Falls section adds modern construction and home designs.

PebbleCreek is known for its guard-gated security (uncommon for the size), country-club amenities, and resort lifestyle. Residents enjoy 54 holes of championship golf across two clubs — Eagle's Nest (18 holes, 6,790-yd par 72) and Tuscany Falls (36 holes across East and West courses, designed by Dick Bailey). The community also boasts 36 pickleball courts, one of the largest pickleball complexes in any AZ 55+ community.

PebbleCreek is the priciest 55+ resort community in the West Valley after Corte Bella — median around $551K, new construction $503K–$970K. Best fit for buyers who want country-club living without the boutique scale of Corte Bella.

📍 Where We Are

PebbleCreek on the map

PebbleCreek sits in Goodyear in Arizona's West Valley — about 2 miles north of I-10 along PebbleCreek Parkway, roughly 25 miles west of downtown Phoenix and a 30-minute drive from Sky Harbor Airport. Nestled between the White Tank Mountains and Estrella Mountain Park.

❓ Real Questions Real Buyers Ask

PebbleCreek Buyer FAQ — Everything You Were Going to Google

Is PebbleCreek really guard-gated?

Yes. PebbleCreek has a guard-gated entrance, which is unusual for a community of 6,100 homes. Most communities its size use unmanned gates or no gates. The guarded entry is part of what justifies the price premium over Sun City Festival or Westbrook Village.

How does PebbleCreek's golf compare to other West Valley communities?

PebbleCreek has 54 holes — two full 18-hole courses (Eagle's Nest and Tuscany Falls), each par 72. Sun City Grand has 4 courses (72 holes total but one is exclusive to Cimarron). Sun City West has 11 courses. PebbleCreek's golf is the most concentrated luxury-resort feel in the West Valley.

Is the new construction at PebbleCreek still active?

Yes. Robson is still selling new homes in PebbleCreek, currently in the Tuscany Falls section. New construction prices run $503K–$970K. The Villa Home Series offers attached low-maintenance options from 1,404–1,903 sqft.

What's the pickleball scene like at PebbleCreek?

PebbleCreek has 36 pickleball courts — one of the largest dedicated complexes among AZ 55+ communities. There's a robust pickleball club with leagues, tournaments, and lessons. If pickleball is a top priority, PebbleCreek is the strongest choice in the West Valley.
