📊 Buyer Budget Guide

What does it really cost to buy in PebbleCreek?

PebbleCreek home prices range from the mid-$400Ks for older resales up to $1M+ for new-construction Estate models. Here's how to think about your real budget — not just the purchase price, but the all-in monthly cost.

PRICE BANDS

Pricing by series

🏠 Resales — Casita Series

$400K-$550KTypical range

MONTHLY CARRY

Sample monthly cost calculations

The purchase price is one thing. Your actual monthly carry includes mortgage (if any), property tax, HOA dues, insurance, and utilities. Here are sample calculations for typical purchase scenarios:

Example 1: $550K cash purchase

  • Mortgage P&I: $0 (cash)
  • Property tax: ~$385/mo (estimated 0.8% annually)
  • HOA dues: ~$214/mo
  • Homeowner's insurance: ~$130/mo
  • Utilities (estimated avg): ~$300/mo
  • Total: ~$1,029/mo carrying cost

Example 2: $700K with $200K down, 30-year mortgage

  • Mortgage P&I (~6.5% rate): ~$3,160/mo
  • Property tax: ~$490/mo
  • HOA dues: ~$214/mo
  • Insurance: ~$165/mo
  • Utilities: ~$350/mo
  • Total: ~$4,379/mo all-in

Example 3: $863K Bacara new build with 50% down

  • Mortgage P&I (~6.5% rate, $431K loan): ~$2,725/mo
  • Property tax: ~$605/mo
  • HOA dues: ~$214/mo
  • Insurance: ~$210/mo
  • Utilities (larger home): ~$425/mo
  • Total: ~$4,179/mo all-in

Important caveats: these are rough estimates for budgeting only. Mortgage rates change. Tax rates depend on assessed value and the year. HOA dues can change. Insurance depends on coverage levels. Use these for ballpark planning, not as quotes.
