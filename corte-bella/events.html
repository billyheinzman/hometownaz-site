Corte Bella events, activities, and community calendar

Corte Bella's calendar runs full year-round — fitness classes daily, weekly card games and crafting groups, monthly chef's dinners, seasonal golf tournaments, and a long list of resident-organized clubs. The official community calendar lives on the residents' portal at cortebella.net (members only — log in with your owner credentials). The golf club's public events are listed openly at cortebellagolfclub.com. Below: links into both systems plus a snapshot of what fills the calendar most months.

🗓️ Resident Calendar (cortebella.net)

MembersLogin required

The official community calendar for Corte Bella residents lives on the HOA portal at cortebella.net. It's powered by FRONTSTEPS (a property management platform) and shows all clubhouse events, fitness classes, club meetings, board meetings, and Architectural Review deadlines.

To access:

  1. Visit cortebella.net
  2. Click Sign In (top right)
  3. Use your owner credentials (set up at closing by the HOA management company)
  4. Navigate to Community Calendar

Don't have credentials yet? New owners receive them within 7-14 days of closing. If you've lost yours, contact the HOA office or call us — we can walk you through it.

⛳ Golf Club Public Events

OpenNo login needed

The Corte Bella Golf Club publishes its tournaments, member-guest events, junior camps, and Grille special dinners openly on their public website. You don't need to be a resident or a member to see what's coming up.

Examples of public events seen on the schedule:

  • Weekly women's and men's leagues
  • Member-guest invitational tournaments (typically March, November)
  • Sunday brunch in the Grille
  • Wine-tasting and chef's table dinners
  • Holiday-themed tournaments (Memorial Day, Independence Day, etc.)
  • Charity outings (open to public play)

🏊 Recurring Weekly Activities

DailyYear-round

A typical week at Corte Bella includes:

  • Fitness classes — yoga, aqua aerobics, strength training, line dancing (multiple sessions daily)
  • Tennis and pickleball — open play sessions plus league nights
  • Bocce ball — outdoor courts, leagues run fall through spring
  • Card games and mahjong — bridge, poker, canasta clubs meet weekly
  • Book clubs and crafting groups — multiple subgroups based on interest
  • Wine and social hours — Friday afternoons in the Grille

The full schedule of fitness classes is posted on the residents' portal each week. The activities coordinator (full-time staff position) curates the calendar based on resident interest.

🎄 Seasonal & Holiday Highlights

MonthlyYear-round

The clubhouse social calendar has a strong rhythm of seasonal events:

  • Winter (Nov–Mar): Snowbird welcome socials, Thanksgiving and Christmas dinners at the Grille, New Year's Eve gala, Valentine's couples dinner
  • Spring (Mar–May): Member-guest golf invitational, Easter brunch, Mother's Day brunch, Memorial Day pool opening
  • Summer (Jun–Aug): Quieter calendar (many residents travel), but fitness classes continue indoors, July 4th BBQ + fireworks viewing
  • Fall (Sep–Oct): Welcome-back-snowbirds events, Oktoberfest, Halloween costume party, fall golf tournaments

🏛️ Board Meetings & HOA

MonthlyOpen to owners

The Corte Bella Master Association Board meets monthly. Meetings are open to all owners and the schedule is published on the cortebella.net members portal. Agendas usually include:

  • Financials and reserve fund updates
  • Architectural Review Committee report
  • Amenity utilization and projects
  • Vendor reviews (landscaping, gate security, pool)
  • Resident input session (open to anyone in attendance)

Board meetings are the primary venue for owners to raise concerns, vote on assessments, and propose rule changes. Attending one or two early in your ownership is one of the fastest ways to understand how the community operates.
