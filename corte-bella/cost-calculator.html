The True Cost Calculator will let you enter a specific Corte Bella home's asking price and get the complete picture: HOA, property tax, insurance, mortgage P&I, one-time fees, and the all-in monthly carrying cost. We're finishing the build now.

💡 Use Buyer Budget for now

Until the True Cost Calculator is live, the Buyer Budget page does almost the same job — enter your monthly budget and cash on hand, and it tells you the max home price you can afford. Most buyers find that more useful anyway.

🔍 Want a specific home costed out?

For a specific listing you're considering, just call us. We'll pull the home up in ARMLS, look at the actual HOA and any sub-HOA dues, run the mortgage math at current rates, and email you a one-page breakdown the same day. No charge, no obligation.

📊 What the calculator will include

When live, this calculator will pull in: list price, current rate environment, property tax for the specific parcel, master HOA, sub-HOA if applicable, insurance estimate, one-time closing fees, and a complete monthly + annual cost breakdown.
