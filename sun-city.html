About Sun City

Where Del Webb built the blueprint for retirement living.

Sun City (zip 85351 / 85373 / 85351) is the genesis of every active-adult community that came after it — including Sun City West, Sun City Grand, and Corte Bella. When Del Webb opened the original Sun City model homes on January 1, 1960, the company sold 237 homes in the first weekend alone.

Today, Sun City offers some of the most affordable entry points in the West Valley for buyers 55+. The community features 7 golf courses, 7 recreation centers, over 120 chartered clubs, and an enormous variety of floor plans across multiple build eras spanning 1960 through the 1980s.

Important: Sun City has its own HOA structure, comp pool, and floor plan inventory — properties here are not directly comparable to Sun City West, Sun City Grand, or Corte Bella.
